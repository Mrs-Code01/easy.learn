KEY PERSONALITIES AND THEIR ROLES IN THE AMALGAMATION OF NIGERIAN TERRITORIES

ROLES OF THE COLONIAL ADMINISTRATORS

01

Taubman Goldie was the father of Nigeria that ascertained the claim of British on Nigerian territories.

02

Taubman Goldie was the founder of the Royal Niger Company who appointed Lord Luggard to unite the two protectorates of North and South together as one colony.

03

Taubman Goldie and Lord Luggard were responsible for the development of Northern Nigeria.

04

Lord Luggard signed a document consolidating the two, thereby creating the colony and protectorate of Nigeria.

ROLES OF THE EUROPEAN MISSIONARIES

1. The missionaries western-style of education influenced our values, attitudes and life style positively.
2. They eradicated slave trade and some barbaric traditions, like killing of twins.

3. They influenced our vision by standing against the colonial masters.
4. They exposed us to western education.

ROLES OF TRADITIONAL RULERS

1. They were bold to sign a treaty that benefited the people

2. They resisted the force of Britain to completely have control over the land of Nigeria.
3. They exercised monopoly over the trade and this was difficult for the colonial administrators to control.
4. Some traditional rulers withheld some vital information from the colonial administration.

ADVANTAGES OF AMALGAMATION IN NIGERIA

1. Diversity in culture and languages.
2. It harnessed natural resources.
3. It brought about economic prosperity.

4. It enhanced human resources / large labour
5. It brought dignity and pride to the country.
6. It created large market for business.
7. It enhanced free movement / exchange of agricultural produce

DISADVANTAGES

1. It led to tribalism.
2. It brought about marginalization
3. It causes clash of interest among ethnic groups.
4. It enhanced the complexity of the country.

5. It brought about the element of strife/disunity.
